Nutella-Oreo Cheesecake

Description

Simple, easy, no-bake Nutella cheesecake with Oreos.

Freeze at least 3 hours and serve cold.

Ingredients

Crust

  • 1.5 cups graham cracker crumbs
  • 0.3 cup packed brown sugar
  • half a teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 0.3 cup unsalted butter, melted, or more as needed

Filling

  • 8oz cream cheese, softened
  • 2 teaspoons lemon juice
  • 2 cups heavy whipping cream
  • 0.3 cup white sugar
  • 14oz package of Oreo
  • 0.75 cup of Nutella

Steps

  1. Mix cracker crumbs, brown sugar, and cinnamon together in a bowl with hands.
  2. Add melted butter and mix until combined. If mixture seems dry, add up to quarter cup more melted butter.
  3. Press the mixture evenly to the bottom of a pan. Chill in the freezer for at least 30 minutes.
  4. Beat cream cheese and lemon juice until soft. Add whipping cream and continue beating until mixture is thick.
  5. Mix in sugar until stiff. Separate mixture into 2 equal amounts.
  6. Crush 18 oreos and fold them into one bowl of filling. Spread the mixture evenly over chilled crust.
  7. Place in the freezer for 30 minutes.
  8. Mix nutella in the second bowl of filling and spread evenly over chilled cookie layer.
  9. Cover and freeze for 3-8 hours and it's ready!
